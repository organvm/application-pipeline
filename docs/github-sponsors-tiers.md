# GitHub Sponsors Tier Structure

## Why Sponsor ORGANVM?

ORGANVM is a 148-repo open-source creative-institutional system built by a solo
artist-technologist. It includes a constitutional corpus (27 specs, 84K+ words),
49 published essays, and infrastructure spanning recursive engines, generative art,
commercial tools, orchestration, and governance -- all released under MIT and
CC-BY-4.0 licenses.

Sponsoring supports the continued development of open infrastructure that
demonstrates how one person can steer automation toward empowerment rather than
collapse. Every dollar sustains the work directly -- there is no institutional
backing, no VC, no grant cushion. Even $5/month starts the revenue counter and
proves the model.

---

## Recurring Tiers

### Tier 1 -- Observer ($5/month)

**For those who want to see the system grow.**

Benefits:
- Name listed in SUPPORTERS.md (maintained in the constitutional corpus)
- Early access to essays before public release
- Sponsor badge on GitHub profile

### Tier 2 -- Contributor ($15/month)

**For those who want to follow the work closely.**

Everything in Observer, plus:
- Monthly System Pulse newsletter (metrics, milestones, architectural decisions)
- Access to constitutional corpus PDFs (27 specs, formatted and versioned)
- Access to sponsor-only discussion threads

### Tier 3 -- Patron ($50/month)

**For those who want a voice in the direction.**

Everything in Contributor, plus:
- Quarterly 30-minute voice call (system walkthrough, Q&A, feedback)
- Input on system direction (vote on quarterly priorities)
- Name in the Patrons section of the stakeholder portal

### Tier 4 -- Benefactor ($150/month)

**For those who want to shape the methodology.**

Everything in Patron, plus:
- Dedicated monthly briefing (written or call, your preference)
- Co-authorship credit on methodology documentation
- Early access to commercial tool betas (ORGAN-III products)
- Named acknowledgment in published papers and conference talks
